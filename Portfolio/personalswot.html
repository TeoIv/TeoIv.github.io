By Teodor Ivanov

Sep 11, 2021

In this article I will try to answer the following questions:

What do you do best? What are your positive traits? What personality traits may be holding you back in your studies / career? How can you turn your strengths / weaknesses into opportunities? What obstacles do you face to succeed as a student? Could any of your weaknesses prevent you from succeeding in your studies / career?

Well, I am very disciplined and determined and when someone sets the bar too high my goal is to jump over that bar. My communication level is waaaay above average and I believe that this is going to help me a lot in the new environment.

A negative aspect of my personality is probably the laziness. Its not an excuse, but I think this is based on the fact that im in my comfort zone when im home, so I believe with my arrival in Middleburg I will work hard on improving in every aspect. My communication skills open a lot of doors and I hope they keep helping me, but now turning my weaknesses into opportunities is going to be a challenge, but there is a saying "it takes a lazy man to find the easiest way out".
