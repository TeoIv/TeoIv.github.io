My Programming Experience

By Teodor Ivanov

Sep 10, 2021

Ever since I was 15, I have always been fascinated by how games are made and what goes behind the development of a software. Mathematics has always been my favorite subject in school, and I used to picture the equations that I had to solve as real-life problems. For this reason, I chose to study advanced mathematics as bonus classes in school, which has substantially developed my analytical skills.

I discovered my interest in IT in high school, so I started researching Front- and Back-end Web-Development and that is how my interest in the modern world of technology appeared. As a result, I have taken up a short Web-Development course, in order to expand my knowledge in programming.
